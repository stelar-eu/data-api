Welcome to STELAR KLMS

The STELAR project designs and develops a Knowledge Lake Management System (KLMS) for turning raw data lakes into knowledge lakes. This combination of a platform and its tools will also hold the purpose of simple and intelligent data discovery, AI-ready data, and semantic interoperability in smart agriculture and food safety applications. Through all of this, STELAR will embrace the FAIR (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable) approach to data.
